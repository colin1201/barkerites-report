Tap to choose your game file
or drag it here · accepts .gpx and .tcx
🔒 Processed on your device — your file is never uploaded or stored anywhere.

How to get your file (~1 minute)

Garmin: open connect.garmin.com in your phone's browser (the export lives on the website, not the app) → log in → open the game activity → tap the gear icon → Export to GPX. The file downloads — come back here and drop it in.
Strava: open the activity on strava.com → ••• menu → Export GPX.
No file? You need to record the game on a GPS sports watch (Garmin, Coros, Suunto, Apple Watch via a GPX export app).
Minutes into your recording. Started recording during warm-up? Drag kick-off forward so quarters and the heat map cover only the game. Halftime sets where the teams swapped ends — auto-guessed, drag if the map looks mirrored/smeared.
